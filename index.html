The Biography

Adrian Parrilla, operating under the moniker just be nice., is an independent artist and visionary based in the Southern California music scene. Rooted in Indie R&B, modern atmospheric production, and vintage nostalgia.

Beyond his solo work, he is the Director of the Yermo South West Connection (YSWC) media branch and hosts the indie music publication, let's just be friends.

Event Review

MLB BREEZEE at The Yost

March 7, 2026 • Words by Adrian Parrilla

The Yost Theater felt entirely different on Thursday night. From the moment MLB BREEZEE stepped onto the stage, the atmosphere shifted. It wasn't just a performance; it was a masterclass in West Coast energy and crowd control. Blending razor-sharp lyricism with booming, inescapable bass lines, BREEZEE commanded the room effortlessly.

The highlight of the night was undoubtedly the live rendition of his latest single, where the crowd's energy perfectly mirrored the track's intensity. If you weren't paying attention to the Inland Empire/OC underground before, MLB BREEZEE just gave you a reason to start.
